Meet Our Founder

Sakshi Tripathi

Software Engineer at Qualtrics · Founder, TechWave Global

Sakshi Tripathi is a Software Engineer at Qualtrics and the founder of TechWave Global. With dual Master's degrees in Computer Science — an MS from Texas State University (where she received the Graduate Academic Excellence Award) and an Integrated M.Tech from Gautam Buddha University — Sakshi brings deep technical expertise to her mission of democratizing AI education.

Her journey spans building scalable systems at Amazon and Qualtrics, teaching 100+ students as an Assistant Professor at PSIT Kanpur, and ranking in the top percentiles nationally (GATE AIR 1642, UGC NET/JRF 99.3%ile). It was this unique blend of industry experience and passion for teaching that led her to found TechWave Global in October 2024.

Her mission: to make Tier 2 and Tier 3 Indian cities AI-literate and fluent through an innovative offline/online hybrid model — bringing world-class AI education to students who need it most.

Techwave Global AI Academy is a Joint Venture of TechWave Global LLC (Washington, USA) & Indrashikha Educational (OPC) Pvt Ltd
